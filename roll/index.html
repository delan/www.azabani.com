A selection of blogs and other websites that I find interesting enough to follow avidly.

  • Fabrice Bellard's Home Page by Fabrice Bellard
    Creator of FFmpeg and QEMU and genius hacker who won the IOCCC twice.
  • The Old New Thing by Raymond Chen
    A veteran's struggle to keep Windows backwards compatible over the decades.
  • Dave's Archives by Dave Cooper
    A software engineering lecturer with a keen sense for Australian politics.
  • Vi Hart by Vi Hart
    Mathemusician illustrating everything from hexaflexagons to toothpaste usage.
  • The Slightly Disgruntled Scientist by Jason Heeris
    Engineer, physicist and staunch advocate for the extinguishment of the sun.
  • xkcd by Randall Munroe
    “A webcomic of romance, sarcasm, math, and language.”
  • Kye Russell by Kye Russell
    Linux, Apple hardware and code plus a few transcripts with Richard Stallman.
  • Joel on Software by Joel Spolsky
    Words by a master software engineer and co-creator of Stack Overflow.
  • Raw Thought by Aaron Swartz
    Uncensored and heterogenous writing from a programmer and visionary activist.
